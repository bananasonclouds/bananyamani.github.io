🧪 Research

I’m a physics master's student at the University of Zurich, focusing on experimental cosmology. My current research explores fiber positioner arrays for galaxy surveys—developing tools to improve redshift measurements and map large-scale structure more precisely.

I’m especially interested in Cosmic Microwave Background B-modes, baryon acoustic oscillations, and bridging hands-on instrumentation with theoretical insight.

🚀 Astronautics & Space Advocacy

I'm passionate about space exploration and making astrophysics accessible to Arabic-speaking communities. I'm in ongoing conversations with the Saudi Space Commission and committed to opening pathways for the next generation of scientists and explorers from the region.

🎙️ Podcast: Spacebites with Benny

Spacebites with Benny is my bilingual podcast (Arabic & English) that simplifies deep space topics—from galaxies to gravitational waves—through metaphors, stories, and curiosity. It’s science with heart and rhythm.
